KERALA

Top Destinations

Backwater

The backwater regions of Kerala are one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. The tranquil backwater cruises are a once in a lifetime experience. Alappuzha, known as the 'Venice of the East', is especially popular for its houseboat cruises where you can soak in nature at its finest form.

Wildlife

There are a number of popular wildlife sanctuaries in Kerala housing exotic and rare species of flora and fauna. Nestled in the elaborate forest of the majestic Western Ghats or Sahyadri Ranges in Kerala are fourteen wildlife sanctuaries and two tiger reserves.

Monuments

Kerala monuments are relics of a past that the state is extremely proud of. These indicate some of the finest gifts left for us by the ancestors.

Beaches

As Kerala is a coastal state, some of the most popular tourist destinations in Kerala are beaches like Kovalam, Varkala, Chowara, Chavakkad, Nattika, Cherai, Kizhunna, Poovar and the list goes on and on.

Festivals

Our festivals celebrate the traditions passed down by our ancestors. Be it Onam, Eid or Christmas, all of Kerala rejoices with one spirit to bring the entire coastal State to life.

Hills

Kerala brings to you a magical tryst with nature through its various hills and hill stations. A majority of these places in Kerala are situated on the windward side of the majestic Western Ghats.

Waterfalls

Kerala is popular for having numerous majestic waterfalls. These scintillating cascades are famous picnic spots and excursion destinations round the year.

Museums

Museums are storehouses of not just our history but also a treasure trove of information regarding the various events and mechanisms that led us to this point in time.

Climate

Visiting this piece of paradise along the Malabar Coast offers a mixed of experiences to the visitors seasons throughout the year.
